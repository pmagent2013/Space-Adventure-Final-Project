Instructions to play Space Adventure

Exploring the World

When an NPC or your character is conversing, simply click "Next" to proceed throughout the game. To play the game, select an action that is available for each area.

Actions

Explore Explores the area for other locations.
Search Searches the area for items that can be found.
Talk Talks to an NPC that is in the area.
Teleport Teleports the main character on and off the ship.
Hyperdrive Allows the character to travel to a new planet.
Weapon System Displays the weapons available to use. (Only available in Control Room)


Hints!

  • Make sure to pay attention to hints in the dialogue to obtain necessary items to continue the game!
  • Also make sure to search every area and talk to every NPC.
  • If it seems that every option has been exhausted, then you didn't obtain a necessary item and you lost!

    • Please restart the game and try again.
